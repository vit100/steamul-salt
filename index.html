Uncover Health & Rejuvenation in Salt Palace Spa

Salt Palace Spa offers a unique 450sq.f. salt room fully made with beautiful pink Himalayan salt.
Himalayan salt is rock salt from the Punjab region of Pakistan, near the Himalayas. This salt deposit comes from a sea present during the Permian and Cretaceous eras, around 250 million years ago. This sea apparently ended up landlocked, evaporating until it left a dense salt deposit, colored by a common pink microorganism that had lived in it. Himalayan salt is 100% natural and drug free product, which contains about 84 trace elements valuable to our bodies including iodine, magnesium and potassium.

New! Purifying Himalayan Salt Body Treatment.

To build the room we incorporated around 10 tons of salt. Due to high amount of rock Himalayan salt the microclimate in our room is pretty identical to those natural salt caves found in the mountains. The healing effect is increased with use of special state-of-the-art halo generator, which injects a dry salt aerosol into the salt room so you can realize the full benefits of our Himalayan salt in your lungs and on your skin. Because the salt is ground to a microscopic consistency, it can penetrate the deepest recesses of your lungs and skin, which is where the real work takes place.
